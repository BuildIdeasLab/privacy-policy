Privacy Policy for Startup Validation Tool

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

Startup Validation Tool ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

  1. Startup Ideas: When you use the app, you may enter descriptions of your startup ideas. This information is stored locally on your device.
  2. Validation Data: The app collects your responses to validation questions and criteria you provide when evaluating your ideas.
  3. Progress Data: The app tracks your progress through the MVP checklist and task completion data, stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  1. Device Information: We may collect basic device information necessary for app functionality (iOS version, device model).
  2. Usage Data: The app may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage

  • All your ideas, validation data, and progress information are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.
  • We do not store your personal data on our servers.
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

1. OpenAI API

  • When you validate an idea or generate a PDF report, your idea description and validation criteria are sent to our backend server.
  • Our backend server then sends this data to OpenAI's API to generate AI-powered feedback and reports.
  • We do not store your ideas on our servers - they are processed and then discarded.
  • OpenAI's use of data is governed by their privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

2. Backend Server

  • Our backend server (hosted on Vercel) acts as a secure intermediary between the app and OpenAI.
  • Your data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.
  • Data is not stored on our backend servers - it is processed and immediately discarded.

Subscription Information

  • If you purchase a subscription, payment information is processed by Apple through the App Store.
  • We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data as necessary:

  1. OpenAI: Idea descriptions are sent to OpenAI API for processing (via our secure backend)
  2. Apple: Subscription information is managed through Apple's App Store
  3. Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law

Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Your data is stored locally on your device
  • We use secure backend servers with proper authentication
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely on our backend, not in the app

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access: View all data stored in the app (accessible through the app interface)
  2. Delete: Delete your ideas and data at any time through the app
  3. Export: Export your ideas and reports (PDF generation feature)
  4. Uninstall: Remove all data by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: build@ideaslabglobal.co.uk

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Backend Processing: Data sent to our backend for AI processing is not stored and is immediately discarded after processing
  • OpenAI: Subject to OpenAI's data retention policies (see their privacy policy)

Location Data

We do not collect or use location data.

Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not include personally identifiable information.

International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.
